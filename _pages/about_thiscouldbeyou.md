The CAMP Lab is recruiting **undergraduate RAs, a full-time lab manager (post-bacc), graduate students, and postdoctoral researchers**. Graduate applicants may apply through the [Psychological & Brain Sciences Graduate Program](https://psychology.uiowa.edu/graduate) or the [Neuroscience Graduate Program](https://neuroscience.grad.uiowa.edu). The deadline is Dec 1, 2026.

Postdoctoral applicants may apply [here](/assets/html/postdoc_application.html). (view [job description](/assets/pdf/Yee_Lab_Postdoc_Job_Ad.pdf)).  
Post-bacc applicants may apply [here](/assets/html/postbacc_application.html) (view [job description](/assets/pdf/Yee_Lab_PostBacc_Job_Ad.pdf)).

We welcome candidates with backgrounds in cognitive or affective neuroscience, computational modeling, data science, computer science, or clinical science. The CAMP Lab is committed to **inclusive excellence and mutual respect** — we believe diverse perspectives make for better science.
