We are actively recruiting at all levels — **undergraduate RAs, graduate students, and postdoctoral researchers**. Graduate applicants are welcome through either the [Department of Psychological and Brain Sciences](https://psychology.uiowa.edu) or the [Neuroscience Program](https://neuroscience.uiowa.edu) at the University of Iowa.

We are especially excited to hear from candidates with backgrounds in **cognitive neuroscience, clinical science, or computational methods** (Python, R). The CAMP Lab is committed to **inclusive excellence and mutual respect** — we believe diverse perspectives make for better science.

If you are interested in joining the team, please reach out to <a href="mailto:debbie_yee@brown.edu">Dr. Yee</a> with your **CV** and a brief description of your research interests.
