**Dr. Debbie Yee** is the Director of the Computational Affective Motivational Psychiatry Lab. She received her B.S. in Brain and Cognitive Sciences at MIT and her Ph.D. in Psychological and Brain Sciences at Washington University in St. Louis. After completing her postdoctoral training at Brown University, she will move to the University of Iowa (Go Hawkeyes!) to launch her lab focused on mechanisms of motivation, affect, and decision-making, with an eye towards translating mechanistic frameworks of transdiagnostic processes to the neural and computational basis of mental health.
